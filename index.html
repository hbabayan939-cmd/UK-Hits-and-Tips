Arsenal Breakdown

Weapons Guide

Every gun in ULTRAKILL has a primary fire, alternate fire, and secrets to unlock. Swapping weapons resets the style penalty — never stop cycling.

Revolver
Primary Arm · Blue / Red / Green
The revolver is your most versatile weapon. High damage per shot and coin tricks make it lethal at any range. Each variant unlocks a new trick shot mechanic that chains into massive style combos.
Pro Tip Shoot a coin mid-air and it reflects bullets at the nearest enemy — chain multiple coins for one-shot boss chunks.
Shotgun
Primary Arm · Blue / Red / Green
Close-range devastation. The pump shotgun also functions as a movement tool — fire it downward while in the air to gain extreme vertical height. Core Eject variant launches a fiery module before reloading.
Pro Tip Shotgun jumping (firing down mid-air) is one of the primary mobility tools — combine it with a dash for massive map traversal.
Nailgun
Primary Arm · Blue / Red / Green
Sustained suppression fire perfect for melting crowds. The Attractor variant pulls enemies together with magnetic nails — set it up, then fire your heavy weapons into the cluster for devastating damage.
Pro Tip The Sawblade alt-fire ricochets off walls. Bounce it into a group for incredible style point generation.
Railcannon
Heavy Arm · Blue / Red / Green
Charged single-shot weapon that penetrates all enemies in a line. The Electric Railcannon generates a chained lightning strike — use it near clusters to arc massive damage across multiple targets.
Pro Tip Fire the Railcannon through a Malicious Face beam to instantly destroy it, saving you from the most dangerous hazard in the game.
Rocket Launcher
Heavy Arm · Blue / Red / Green
Explosive area denial powerhouse. Rockets can be frozen mid-air using the Freeze Frame ability and punched or shot into targets. The S.R.S. Canon variant fires a tracking rocket that homes on marked targets.
Pro Tip Freeze a rocket, then punch it (Whiplash or Knuckleblaster) to deal explosive melee damage in a massive burst — great for staggered bosses.
Feedbacker
Arm Slot · Blue Variant
Your most essential tool for survival. Parrying projectiles and enemy attacks with Feedbacker reflects them back with triple damage and restores health. The timing window is forgiving — learn it immediately.
Pro Tip Parrying a red-glowing skull projectile sends it back homing on its source — critical for keeping your health bar topped off throughout an encounter.

Combat Manual

Tips & Hits

Essential tactics for dominating every encounter. Speed, style, and survival — prioritised in that order.

01
Never Stop Moving
ULTRAKILL punishes stationary players brutally. Dash, slide, jump, and rocket jump constantly. The game's AI tracks your last known position, so being in motion makes you near-impossible to hit consistently.
Movement
02
Weapon Swap for Style
Using the same weapon repeatedly tanks your style meter fast. Cycle through all available weapons in a fight — even a single shot from a secondary weapon resets the style decay timer on your primary.
Style
03
Parry Everything
The Feedbacker arm parries any yellow-glowing or red-glowing projectile. A successful parry heals you with reflected blood and sends the attack back tripled in power. Against fast projectile-heavy enemies, parrying is your main healing source.
Survival
04
Kill Fast, Heal Faster
Blood pools drop on enemy death and slowly disappear. You absorb them by walking over them. The faster you kill, the more blood is on the floor, the more you can heal. Aggressive play is self-sustaining play.
Survival
05
Prioritise Dangerous Targets
Malicious Faces, Mindflayers, and Virtues are the most threatening enemies in any mixed encounter. Always focus burst-damage weapons on these first. Leave slower melee enemies for last — they're easy to kite while you mop up.
Priority
06
Use the Environment
Explosive barrels, acid pools, and floor hazards count toward your style score. Launching an enemy into a hazard gives unique kill medals. Some arenas are designed specifically around environmental kills — look for them.
Style
07
Slam for Ground Pounds
Holding slide mid-air causes V1 to slam down with a powerful ground stomp. This deals heavy damage in an AoE, launches airborne enemies, and can be chained into a slide-dash for immediate repositioning.
Movement
08
Learn Boss Patterns Cold
Every boss in ULTRAKILL has a repeating attack pattern with very readable telegraphs. Before you try to P-rank a boss, spend one run simply observing and dodging. Once the patterns are memorised, you can focus purely on offense.
Boss Fights
09
Whiplash Pulls & Punches
The Whiplash arm pulls small enemies toward you for a brutal close-range combo, and pulls you toward large ones. Use it to drag shielded enemies out of position and follow up instantly with a shotgun or railcannon.
Advanced

Threat Assessment

Enemy Breakdown

Know what you're fighting before it kills you. Weaknesses, behaviours, and priority ratings for every type.

Enemy Threat Key Behaviour Weakness / Counter
Filth Easy Charges directly. Very slow. Low health pool. Any weapon. Shotgun one-shots early game.
Drone Easy Hovers, fires slow tracking bullets. Groups are annoying. Revolver headshots or nailgun burst. Parry their bullets back.
Soldier Medium Fires shotgun blasts and retreats. Will take cover. Aggressive pressure prevents their retreat. Railcannon through walls.
Streetcleaner Medium Launches flamethrower streams. Explosive when killed. Shoot from medium range. Don't get caught in the death explosion.
Mindflayer Hard Teleports constantly, spawns projectile walls. Very aggressive. Railcannon one-tap during teleport stun. Don't let it dictate distance.
Malicious Face Hard Fires a sustained death beam. Tracks movement predictively. Shoot through the beam with Railcannon to instantly delete it.
Virtue Hard Shields nearby enemies. Fires electric bolts. Priority target. Destroy the shield with a charged attack, then burst it down fast.
Hideous Mass Hard Massive tank. Arms flail in AoE. Hard to stagger. Railcannon core shots. Rocket launcher clusters. Stay airborne.
V2 (Boss) Boss Mirrors your own moveset and weapon usage. Parries your shots. Coins distract it. Punish with heavy arms while its parry is recovering.
Gabriel (Boss) Boss Extremely fast, multi-phase. Quotes scripture while killing you. Learn every pattern. Parry the glowing sword lunges. Railcannon between combos.
